Project Objective:

ABC Company currently manages employee expense vouchers manually. Employees create vouchers, obtain approval from the Director, and submit them to the Accounts Team for reimbursement.

Develop a web-based Expense Voucher Management System that digitizes the complete voucher creation, approval, and reimbursement workflow.

The application should:
- Replace the manual voucher process.
- Allow employees to create and submit vouchers.
- Allow Directors to approve or reject vouchers.
- Allow Accounts Team to process approved vouchers.
- Allow employees to track voucher status.
- Maintain a centralized repository.
- Use role-based authentication and authorization.

Expense Date required.
Amount > 0.
Employee Signature required before submit.

Business Rules

Voucher Number unique.
Voucher Number auto generated.
New voucher starts as Draft.
Employee edits only Draft.
Submitted vouchers become read-only.
Approved vouchers become read-only.
Only Director approves/rejects.
Employee sees only own vouchers.
